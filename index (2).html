SOFIA LAMC is a concept-stage company building robotic prospecting and resource-data infrastructure for the lunar and cis-lunar economy. We begin by mapping Helium-3 and volatile concentrations and licensing that data to space agencies and fusion-energy developers, generating revenue years before any extraction hardware flies. From there, we advance toward a robotic extraction demonstration once resource maps and mission partnerships are in place.

The Opportunity

A Supply-Constrained Market
With Confirmed Demand

Helium-3 is priced at $20M/kg with virtually no global supply. Fusion reactor programs are ready to buy. SLAM builds the supply chain.

Initial Wedge Market

Resource-data licensing — not extraction — is SOFIA LAMC's point of entry. The four-satellite spectrometer constellation can fly and generate licensable lunar resource maps years before any extraction hardware reaches the surface, giving the company a near-term, diligence-friendly revenue line ahead of the larger He-3 extraction opportunity.
