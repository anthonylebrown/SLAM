Mission & Technology

Solving Earth's Resource Crisis
from Beyond the Atmosphere

Sofia addresses the structural scarcity of critical materials by deploying proven, mission-qualified systems to extract and deliver high-value resources from the Moon and near-Earth asteroid bodies.

The Problem

Global Demand Is Outpacing Terrestrial Supply

Global demand for electricity is accelerating substantially due to data center growth, AI infrastructure, domestic industrial expansion, and widespread electrification of transportation and industrial processes. Existing renewable systems remain intermittent and land-intensive, creating growing pressure on power grids and continued dependence on fossil fuels for reliable baseload energy. The growing possibility of a supply-demand imbalance results in a projected cost of electricity that exceeds the rate of inflation — impacting residential, commercial, and industrial users across every sector of the global economy.

The Solution

Space-Based Resource Extraction at Scale

Sofia's integrated resource architecture combines advanced deployable mechanisms, precision antenna systems, and space-qualified hardware to capture solar energy beyond Earth's atmosphere, beam it safely and efficiently to the surface, and deliver it as baseload electricity to utilities, industries, and critical infrastructure.

  • 24/7 baseload renewable energy generation, weather-independent
  • Modular, scalable SolarSat constellation architecture
  • Tenth of the land footprint required by terrestrial solar
  • Proven zero-failure delivery record across 40+ spaceflight missions
  • Regulatory-compliant operations under international spectrum frameworks
Core Capabilities

Mission-Critical Space Hardware & Systems

⚙️

Deployable Mechanism Systems

Advanced compact flexible array stowing and deployment systems, deploying up to 4,000 m² of surface from each mission platform. Proven on NASA programs including Europa Clipper and James Webb Space Telescope support missions.

📡

Advanced Antenna & Radar Systems

Space-qualified antenna arrays and deployable radar systems engineered for reliability and high-volume deployment. Active on programs including SUNRISE CubeSat Constellation Antennas and Europa Clipper Deployable Radar Antennas.

🔭

Precision Sensor Arrays

Deployable sensor systems for planetary lander and surface prospecting missions, enabling real-time subsurface analysis. Engineered for harsh thermal and radiation environments of lunar and deep-space proximity operations.

🏭

Scalable Space Manufacturing

Fully operational 20,000 sq ft facility in Berkeley, CA with high-bay assembly, cleanroom, thermal-vacuum chamber, and precision machine shop. Designed for high-volume production of space-qualified hardware components.

🛰️

Mission Systems Integration

End-to-end mission architecture services from design through delivery, supporting NASA, commercial space firms, and academic institutions. Hardware and services on five active space missions, with two additional missions launching through 2026 and beyond.

📜

Intellectual Property Licensing

Proprietary space technologies and critical systems licensed to commercial partners and government agencies, generating capital-efficient, scalable recurring revenue independent of hardware production cycles. Multiple SBIR Phase 1 and 2 awards secured.
